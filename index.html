Our Profile

Business Analytics

Our business analysts are able to provide consultancy services on a daily, weekly or long-term basis. We can provide additional resource to your existing team to manage workflow peaks, or, if your team requires support we can provide 'at-the-desk' coaching during key milestones to help you meet goals.

Creative UX/UI Designers

Our design team is a small design studio within a large software company that will help you build an engaging product easily and quickly. Want to build your product with a team that establishes a clear design process, meets deadlines, and delivers a spot-on end result? Turn to DXC’s UI and UX Design services.

Software Architects

Our architects provide software architecture services to affect positively time-to-market by creating a working ecosystem, maintaining a global vision throughout all the phases of your projects. We can help you with architecture as well as provide clear technical guidelines regarding your business needs.

Full/Stack Developers

Custom software serves the unique processes of your business, solves your specific problems, satisfies your exclusive needs and makes your workflows easier, faster, and more efficient. On the cutting edge of software development, we continuously master our skills in all tech trends.

Quality Assurance

We offer a variety of software quality assurance services to measure the quality of the product, and quality control services to ensure that products meet customer expectations. Our end goal is to make sure that your software function according to your requirements and specifications and satisfy market demand.

DevOps

Our devops services helping you deliver applications at the pace of business and shift to a continuous delivery model. The ability to roll out improved business capabilities continuously has become essential in today's digital world. Automating end to end delivery pipeline across cloud platforms for faster time to market, increased efficiency and reduced cost.
